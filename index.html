Who We Are
K12 Safety is presented to you by K12 Service Connect, an organization developing software for all aspects of well-being in schools. We have evolved as a partnership between the Missouri School Boards`'` Association and Therapylog.
Missouri School Boards`'` Association
MSBA is a private, nonprofit organization with the vision statement of: Helping School Boards Ensure All Students Succeed. We provide legal assistance, training, and policy support for member school districts in the state of Missouri (most school districts)
Therapylog
Therapylog brings together educators, engineers and advocacy professionals. We build software that makes life easier for school staff and students, support our users and learn from them.
